# ClawLearning

## About
ClawLearning is a free, open educational website about Artificial Intelligence, Large Language Models (LLMs), and AI Agents. The course is designed for adults with no prior AI knowledge who want to understand AI fundamentals and build their own AI agent using OpenClaw.

## Course Structure
The site contains a 30-lesson course spanning 6 weeks, available in Catalan, Spanish, and English:

- Week 1 (Lessons 1-5): AI Fundamentals — What is AI, history, types, models, daily applications
- Week 2 (Lessons 6-10): LLMs — What is an LLM, tokens, context window, current models, prompts
- Week 3 (Lessons 11-15): Infrastructure — Providers, APIs, free vs paid, local models with Ollama
- Week 4 (Lessons 16-20): Agents — What is an agent, components, tools/skills, memory, frameworks
- Week 5 (Lessons 21-25): OpenClaw Practical — Installation, configuration, Telegram, system prompt
- Week 6 (Lessons 26-30): Putting the Agent to Work — Skills, cron automations, multi-agent, security
